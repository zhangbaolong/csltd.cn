宠物当家

物联网智能家居app

宠物当家是一个适合于各种不同基础包括零基础的UX作品集项目教程，你将有一个属于你自己的交互设计作品集项目，该项目的最终成果是一个控制智能家居的移动端app。该项目集UX设计理论、软件使用、项目应用于一体。与此同时，在项目过程中你会学习物联网智能家居产品设计技巧，为你的作品集添加独有项目经验。

概览

Overview

Treats is an app that helps pet owners to take care of their pets when they are not at home. Nowadays, young professionals treat their pets like family members. And it is often worrisome to them that they would not know how their pets are doing when home the pets are home alone. So treat is here to help them monitor, interact and even feed the pets.

设计过程

Design Process

 
 

Video records

Using ai technology, treat’s camera would record special movement of the pets and store them in cloud. Pet owners can trace back and even share the videos if there is a nice one. Also the system is embedded with noise detector, which will monitor the interior environment. If anything odd happens, the app will notify the user.

It all begins with an idea. Maybe you want to launch a business. Maybe you want to turn a hobby into something more. Or maybe you have a creative project to share with the world.
